RICHARD'S IMAGINE CENTER OF HOPE (RICH) AT A GLANCE.

Here at Richard's Imagine Center of Hope (RICH), we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Our decision making process is informed by talking to people who are looking for a second or third time to get their life in perspective. We strive to build productive relationships and make a positive impact in the pursuit of happiness.

COMMUNITY CONNECTION

Richard’s Imagine Center of Hope (RICH) is a start-up not for profit community organization in the City of Pittsburgh. It is designed to provide housing, counseling, and education/employment support to youth throughout Allegheny County. The program’s mission is to provide temporary housing, counseling, mentoring, and educational/ vocational support to youth under 21 who are homeless, dropouts, and unemployed with a reality of becoming incarcerated or end up in the child welfare, mental health, or junvenile/adult justice systems. The vision is to increase young people’s access to safe and stable housing, school/employment connections, enhance social and emotional well-being, self-sufficiency, helping individuals identify their capabilities and build their careers, and work, towards building permanent lasting relationships with families, communities, schools, and other social networks, all positive of course.

YOUTH DEVELOPMENT

RICH’s target market is at-risk young ages 13-21 who dropped out of school, lives on the streets, faces teen parenting, and has been exposed to neglect, violence, and/or trauma. The intentional market is low to moderate income households, businesses, non-profit organizations, and government entities within Allegheny County, City of Pittsburgh. Services will consist of job training/placement, housing security, community development, educational/vocational training, counseling, mentoring, social skill support, civic engagement, referral support, post-secondary focus, employment options, social skill, relationship building, credit counseling, and gardening/landscaping.
